Chocolate Chip Cookies

These classic homemade cookies are soft, chewy, and filled with gooey chocolate chips. Perfect with a glass of milk!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour and baking soda together.
  3. In another bowl, mix melted butter, sugars, and vanilla. Beat in the eggs.
  4. Gradually stir dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Cool and enjoy with milk!
